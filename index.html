About Me

A full stack developer, self-taught entrepreneur, former accountant, and eternal physics student. Currently, full time working for an e-commerce, on their registration pet system with smart tags, and notifications about lost pets as a full stack developer.
I have a bachelor's degree in accounting and 6 semesters completed in physics but life leads me to other paths. I’ve worked as an accountant for some time, which gave me a business understanding of bureaucracy, taxes, and all that stuff, but it was too boring.
Someday, I decided I wanted more and decided to be a physics. I didn't become some kind of an Albert Einstein, but the physics college improved my English a lot and also improved my capability of abstract thinking by studying difficult ideas, giving me a lot of mathematics, calculus, and linear algebra knowledge. By this time, I started selling WordPress websites as a freelancer.
In 2018 I started an e-commerce store called RG para Pets, where we sell tags for pets and decorative IDs. Soon, e-commerce became a pet registration system. Because of this, I quit physics. Today, I’m working to improve this e-commerce system with more features and performance improvements. I’m responsible for the maintenance of the website, updates, UX/UI design, email campaigns, traffic sources, social media, and other stuff too (basically everything).
I was 16 when I got my first job, the world changed a lot from there and all my different experiences drove me here with a lot of baggage. I’m always looking for improvement, and not afraid to learn. While trying to improve the RG para Pets platform, I've learned to program and discovered a whole new world.
Now I feel it's time to move forward! Lets chat!
